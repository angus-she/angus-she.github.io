Registered Address Service

In accordance with the Companies Ordinance of Hong Kong, limited companies need to have a registered office address in Hong Kong for maintaining statutory records.

We provide registered office address service specifically for entrepreneurs who set up their companies in Hong Kong without an office. You are welcomed to use our office address for registration and communication purposes. We can also provide mail collection services to help you with your business growth.
